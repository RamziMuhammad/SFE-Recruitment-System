Discover your Personality !

Our aim is to predict a candidate's scores on the 5 Big Personality Traits

Learn more about us

Our goal is to help jop applicants know their personality based on Big 5 personality Traits.

A short human-centered video is given as input and the output expected to be :

  • Extraversion
  • Agreeableness
  • Conscientiousness
  • Neuroticism
  • Openness
